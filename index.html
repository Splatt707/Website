Super Smash Bros. Brawl

The 3rd installment of the Super Smash Bros series came out in 2008. This game was notable for the huge single player campaign, Subspace Emissary. This game introduced 3rd party characters into the roster, some of the coolest being Snake and Sonic. This game has mixed opinions from fans, casuals love it but competitors dislike it.
